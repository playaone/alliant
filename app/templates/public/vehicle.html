How to get your credit union car loan

Get a preapproved car loan

Submit an online loan application or apply by phone at 800-328-1935 (available 24/7). We will process your application and notify you when your preapproval is completed, usually on the same day.

Complete your loan documents

Be sure to bring your preapproved loan to the dealer, as this will give you maximum leverage and help you negotiate the best deal. Once the deal is finalized, send us the purchase agreement and we can finalize your loan documents using DocuSign.

Finalize your car loan financing

We'll send the funds for free via FedEx 2-business day shipping. Contact your loan consultant if you want it to arrive sooner for a fee (FedEx Overnight Shipping for $18 or FedEx First Overnight Shipping for $22).

Credit union car loan FAQs

How can being preapproved help me when I’m vehicle shopping?

Being preapproved helps you focus on negotiating the best price, knowing that you won’t end up with high rate financing when you close the deal. Plus, dealers tend to take people more seriously when they come in with preapproved financing because it means you’re ready to do business and not just “window shopping.”

How fast can I be approved for a vehicle loan? Can I be approved the same day that I apply?

In many cases, your loan may be approved the same day that you apply. We understand that sometimes the vehicle-buying process is urgent. Our loan specialists will answer your questions and help expedite the simple application and approval process.

How long does it take to get a credit card in the mail?

Once the application is accepted, it typically takes 14-21 days to receive your new Alliant Visa credit card in the mail.

Can I pay off my loan quicker than the payment schedule?

Yes. At Alliant, there are no prepayment penalties for paying off your loan faster than your payment schedule. In fact, it’s a great way to save on interest over the long run.

Do you offer a debt protection plan?

Yes, we offer a debt protection benefit plan in the event of death, disability or involuntary unemployment. Read about the debt protection plan or speak to your Loan Consultant for additional details

Does Alliant do car lease buyouts?

Alliant lease buyout loans are processed by our partner, Lease Maturity. Rates advertised by Alliant are not applicable. Learn more and start an application on the Lease Maturity site.
